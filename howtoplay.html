How To Play

  1. Spin the wheel to get a topic.
  2. Answer 10 set of questions that related to the topic. For each correct question, you’ll get points between 50-300 (depends on the difficulty).
  3. After 10 questions, you’ll get to spin the wheel again.
  4. When your accumulated points reach 1000, you’ll get a star, and you’ll have a chance to spin the wheel again.
  5. Collect 3 stars and you’ve reached the maximum achievement for a topic
  6. Click on the ‘see my badges’ button to see all stars that you’ve collected on each topic
